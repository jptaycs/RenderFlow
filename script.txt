Your neighbor paid $20,000 to put solar panels on his roof. He was told he would be energy independent. Then the first winter bill came and he still owed the power company money. He sold them his summer sunshine at their cheap price and they sold it back to him in December at their expensive price. And somehow at the end of the year, the man with $20,000 of panels on his roof was still writing a check to the utility every month.
